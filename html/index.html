Elderly Population in Italy over the years

The following visualizations want to explore the number of elder people (65+ years) in Italy, focusing on the time interval between 2016 and 2020, with the chance of seeing if and how Covid19 influenced their mortality rate.
Further in the dashboard we will also consider as an underlying health condition the smoking status of the elderly, since the virus mainly affects the lower respiratory tract.
Another interesting point of view can be looking at the monthly average expenditure with regards to healthcare, to see if and how Covid19 concurred in this aspect, too.
Also, the dashboard intends to provide a visualization for the same time interval of the number of deaths in the italian regions, along with their causes, to see how much the virus was involved in it. (Data available in the interval Jan-Apr)
On the left a map of Italy, with the possibility to switch year, showing the number of people over 65 (years) (data from Eurostat).
